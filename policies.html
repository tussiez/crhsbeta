Club Policies

Students must:
  • Adhere to the CRHS Handbook and Beta Pillars.
  • Maintain a 3.8 weighted GPA to remain in the Beta Club.
  • Submit their required 20 service hours to InnerView by April 15, 2027.
  • NOT recieve ISS, OSS, or a referral to Oak Grove Academy.
  • NOT partake in any form of plagirism or academic dishonesty.
  • NOT not miss more than 2 consecutive meetings without a Sponsor-approved excuse.

Volunteer Guidelines

The CRHS Beta Club Sponsors have established guidelines on approved and unapproved service hours.
Read them carefully to avoid your hours being rejected.
